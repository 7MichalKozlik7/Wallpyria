Transform your multi-monitor macOS setup with stunning wallpapers. Create static, animated, and dynamic wallpapers across all your displays with ease. Whether you have a dual-monitor workstation or a complex multi-display setup, Wallpyria gives you complete control over your desktop aesthetics.

---

## Features

### Static Wallpapers
Create perfect wallpapers for each monitor with precise positioning and scaling controls. Set different images per display or use panorama mode to stretch one ultra-wide image seamlessly across multiple monitors.

### Animated Video Wallpapers
Bring your desktop to life with smooth video wallpapers (.mov, .mp4) synchronized across all displays. Full playback controls with loop and quality settings.

### Dynamic HEIC Wallpapers
Time-based wallpapers that change throughout the day, matching sunrise and sunset. Built-in visual timeline editor to create and customize your own dynamic wallpapers.

### Smart Scheduling
Automatically rotate wallpapers on schedule with time-based or solar position triggers. Set different wallpapers for morning, afternoon, evening, and night.

### ML Upscaling
Enhance image quality with machine learning-based upscaling for crisp, sharp wallpapers even from lower resolution sources. Multiple quality presets: Native, High, Super High, Ultra.

### Panorama Mode
Stretch one ultra-wide image seamlessly across multiple monitors for immersive setups. Perfect for landscape photography and wide-angle shots.

### Visual Effects
Blur and artistic effects for login screen, brightness, contrast, and color profile management per monitor. Fine-tune each display independently.

### Multi-Monitor Support
Automatic detection and individual configuration for each display. Works with any number of monitors in any arrangement.
